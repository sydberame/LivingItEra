10,000 Roses

10,000 Roses Description


These roses of about 3 feet tall dance with the sea breeze in the day time and are transformed into magical lights in the night as they are lit at around 6pm every night.
The cafe itself has a viewing deck that can offer a completely amazing view of the Cebu Island, the sea, and of course, the flowers.

Parola Restaurant

Parola Restaurant Description

Parola Seaview Restaurant offers a magnificent view of the sea from sunrise to sunset, in addition to affordable yet delicious dishes.
If you are in Cordoba-Cebu enjoying your vacation at your favorite resort in Mactan Island, you must visit and try this new restaurant,
you will definitely have the perfect place to relax and eat in a very refreshing atmosphere.

Reef Village Resort

RVR Description

Cordova Reef Resort is located off the tourist trail on the southeastern tip of Matican Island.
A yellow metro taxi from the airport takes about 25 minutes depending on traffic and costs about 250pp.
Access to Pier 1 in Cebu cost us 600pp (this includes an additional 100pp taxi fee from the resort) and took about 45 minutes.
The resort has a selection of round villas, the best of which are the pool view villas, as the others do not allow you to see much other than the neighbour's air conditioner.
