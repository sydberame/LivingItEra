10,000 ROSES

The 10,000 Roses Café is a Korean-owned café inspired by South Korea's well-known Dongdaemun Design Plaza.
The café has indoor and outdoor dining areas with modern chairs and tables, LED-powdered roses, and a viewing deck
surrounded by glass walls, making it an Instagram-worthy location. These roses of about 3 feet tall dance with the sea breeze
In the day time and are transformed into magical lights in the night as they are lit at around 6pm every night.
