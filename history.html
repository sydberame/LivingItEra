Sakayan is a basic fisherman's boat in the Cordova. The outrigger is called "katig". Depending on its size, it can carry 1 or more people at a time.
Some "sakayan" evolved through out the years with motorized propeller installed to make the transport more convenient and easier. The boat above is a typical one and uses a paddle.
This was taken in RORO, Cordova, Cebu, Philippines
