BRIC BANK

THE BRIC BANK
IN CODOVA, CEBU

Formerly referred to as the BRICS Development Bank, The BRICS New Development Bank is a multilateral financial institution established by BRICS countries – Brazil, Russia, India, China and South Africa.
The purpose of the bank is to foster the infrastructure and sustainable development efforts in BRICS countries, developing nations and emerging economies.
