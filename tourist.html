10,000 ROSES

THE 10,000 ROSES CAFE'
AT CODOVA, CEBU

a Korean-owned café
inspired by South Korea's well-known Dongdaemun Design Plaza.
The café has indoor and outdoor dining areas with
modern chairs and tables, LED-powdered roses, and a viewing deck
surrounded by glass walls, making it an Instagram-worthy location.
These roses of about 3 feet tall dance with the sea breeze
In the day time and are transformed into magical lights in the night
as they are lit at around 6pm every night.

ENTOY'S BAKASIHAN

THE ENTOY'S BAKASIHAN
AT CODOVA, CEBU

Entoy's Bakasihan is a humble eatery specializing in eel
among other seafood that can make you hungry even if you are not.
It is located at the tip of a docking port in Kamampay Beach
where you can enjoy fresh sea breeze and the best sea view while dining on eel dishes and seafood.
